Welcome to RocketFluid

Developing a user-friendly liquid-fueled rocket engine tailored for enthusiasts involves streamlining the design to enhance accessibility and simplicity of rocket engines. The fundamental components, such as the combustion chamber, oxidizer, and fuel integration into the combustion chamber, need to be constructed using readily available materials and straightforward assembly processes. These processes include copper 3D printing for the nozzle and throat. We also make our fueling system easy to use and very accessible. Prioritizing simplicity extends to the propulsion system and control mechanisms, such as pressure monitoring in the combustion chamber, automated fuel injection, and temperature sensing. Opting for uncomplicated pumps operated by a basic electronic system ensures that enthusiasts, regardless of their technical background, can easily assemble and operate the engine.

In addition to technical considerations, the success of the liquid-fueled rocket engine hinges on comprehensive support and user engagement. Clear, detailed documentation and accessible customer support are integral to empowering users, irrespective of their familiarity with rocketry, to confidently navigate the construction and operation of the engine. A user-friendly interface extends beyond the physical components, encompassing online resources, forums, and collaborative platforms that facilitate a community-driven approach to rocketry. By fostering a dynamic and supportive environment, the aim is not only to provide enthusiasts with a straightforward and exciting product but also to cultivate a space for shared experiences, knowledge exchange, and collaborative innovation within the rocket enthusiast community.
